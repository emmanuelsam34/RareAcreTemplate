The Challenge

The Problems We're Built to Solve

Most costly real estate mistakes happen before a property is acquired, developed, or financed. Our consultancy services help clients make informed decisions and avoid expensive setbacks.

01

Acquiring the Wrong Property

Title issues, ownership disputes, hidden liabilities, and poor investment decisions can lead to costly losses.

02

No Development Direction

Many landowners sit on valuable assets without knowing what to build, what the market demands, or whether a project is financially viable. Clarity starts with a feasibility assessment.

03

Unstructured Projects

Without a proper budget, roadmap, and delivery strategy, projects stall, costs overrun, and developers lose control of their investment before a block is laid.

04

Investing From Abroad Blind

Diaspora investors face unique risks, remote decisions, unreliable agents, no verification, and zero accountability. Our diaspora advisory model is built for this challenge.

05

Hidden Legal Risks

Litigation history, encumbrances, mortgage liens, and community disputes are invisible without proper due diligence. We surface every risk before you commit.

06

Acting Without Expert Advice

Every major real estate decision, buying, building, developing, deserves structured professional guidance. We provide it in a documented, accountable format.

Advisory Process

How a Consultation Works

A structured, documented process from first contact to final report. Designed to protect your interests at every step.

1
Enquiry & Intake

Submit your enquiry online, via WhatsApp, or by direct referral. We capture your property details, objectives, and timeline.

2
Form Completion & Scope Definition

Complete the RareAcre Property Information Form. This enables our team to correctly scope and price your engagement upfront.

3
Invoice & Payment

Receive a clear, itemised engagement invoice. Payment activates your mandate. All terms documented. No hidden fees.

4
Appointment Confirmation

Receive your confirmed appointment details. Sessions conducted in-person at our Lekki Phase 1 office or virtually for diaspora clients.

5
Advisory Sessions & Analysis

Structured advisory sessions are conducted. For verification mandates, field work and registry searches are initiated simultaneously.

6
Report Delivery

Receive your written advisory report, risk assessment, due diligence findings, feasibility analysis, or project plan, fully documented.

7
Recommendation & Next Steps

We walk through the findings together and agree on your next steps, whether acquisition, development commencement, or further advisory.
